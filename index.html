I am actively seeking full-time oppotunities after June 2021.

I am an MPhil student at HKUST, where I work with Prof. Ling Shi. I obtained my bachelor degree at PolyU in June 2019. In the summer of 2018, I worked in Robot Perception Lab at CMU with Prof. Michael Kaess. Prior to that I was a power electronics intern at ASM Pacific Technology.

My research interests lie at control, optimization and robotics. I am able to solve pratical problem with control theory and optimization tools. In particular, I work on optimization-based motion planning for mobile robots, optimal control for positive system and robotic perception. I also have some experiences on state estimation, learning-based control, multi-robot system, and computer vision. Slelected works are listed in Research works and Projects.
